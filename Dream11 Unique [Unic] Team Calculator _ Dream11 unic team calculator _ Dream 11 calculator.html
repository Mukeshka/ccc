What is the formula of team combination?

The formula for calculating the number of possible team combinations is: nCr = n! / r!(n-r)! Where: n = total number of individuals or players r = number of players to be selected for the team ! = factorial, which means the product of all positive integers up to that number. The result of this formula will give you the number of possible team combinations that can be formed from the given pool of individuals or players.
